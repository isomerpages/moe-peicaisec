<p><strong></strong></p><h4><strong>Unity in Technovation: Peicai-Zhonghua ALP Mentorship Programme</strong></h4><p></p>
<p align="justify">select group of our Secondary 2 students have teamed up with Primary 3 to 5 pupils from Zhonghua Primary School in the Unity in Technovation: Peicai-Zhonghua ALP Mentorship Programme. This inaugural interschool partnership empowers our students to mentor younger peers in crafting innovative digital solutions for real-world issues.<br>
For this year's project, our Secondary 2 mentors are guiding these primary pupils in coding and making to develop sustainable living prototypes. These individual projects will come together to form a unified Smart City prototype, emphasising collaboration and collective problem-solving.<br>
Participating in Unity in Technovation equips Peicians with enhanced leadership and mentoring skills, sharpens computational thinking, and nurtures their inventive capabilities. Most importantly, Moreover, it provides our high-achieving ALP students a platform to contribute back, sharing their expertise and guiding their juniors.<br>
