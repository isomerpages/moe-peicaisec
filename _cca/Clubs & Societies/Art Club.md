<h4><strong>Goals &amp; Objectives</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Art Club offers students with interest in visual arts a safe and conducive
space to come together and hone their artistic skills through meaningful
Art projects and competitions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We aim to ensure members get to explore new Art techniques, expand their
knowledge in art and cultivate their spirit of creativity, innovation,
and experimentation through Art.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop member's imagination and creativity through visual representation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide a conducive environment to explore and express their ideas
through Art.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop membersâ€™ ability to express experiences and ideas in their
creation.</p>
</li>
</ol>
