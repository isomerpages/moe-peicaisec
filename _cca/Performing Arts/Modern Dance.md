<h4><strong>Goals &amp; Objectives</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The Modern Dance Club seeks to develop the passion for dancing by providing
our dancers with opportunities to showcase their talents through external
competitions as well as in-house performances culminating to the bi-annual
Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Specializing in contemporary dance which incorporates elements from many
styles of dance such as classical, ballet and jazz.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>During dance trainings, we focus on mastering strong and controlled ballet
legwork, floor work, fall and recovery and other techniques.</p>
</li>
</ol>
