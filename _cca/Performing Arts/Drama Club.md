<p>Peicai Secondary School Drama Club also participated in the Human Values
Drama Festival (HVDF), a meaningful platform that promotes the practice
of the five universal Human Values, Truth, Right Conduct, Peace, Love,
and Non-Violence, through drama and storytelling. The festival provided
students with the opportunity to reflect on these important values while
using performance as a medium to share meaningful messages and inspire
others. Our Secondary 2 and Secondary 3 Drama Club members proudly represented
the school in this event.</p>
<p>The objectives of the festival were to help students develop a deeper
understanding of human values, strengthen their communication and performance
skills, and learn how to work collaboratively as a team. Through the process
of developing their performance, rehearsing together, and preparing for
the presentation, students learnt to appreciate different perspectives
and think critically about the themes portrayed in their story.</p>
<p>Throughout the preparation process, Drama Club members displayed dedication,
perseverance, and strong teamwork. Students supported one another during
rehearsals, encouraged their peers, and worked together to overcome challenges
encountered along the way. The experience also helped them develop important
E21 competencies such as communication and collaboration skills, critical
and adaptive thinking, as well as civic and cross-cultural literacy.</p>
<p>Although the team did not progress beyond the preliminary rounds, the
journey itself was still an enriching and valuable learning experience.
One of the key highlights of the festival was watching students confidently
portray meaningful values and messages through their performance. Students
also grew in confidence, stage presence, self-expression, and their ability
to work closely as an ensemble.</p>
<p>Through opportunities such as HVDF, Peicai Drama Club continues to nurture
students into confident, empathetic, and resilient individuals. Beyond
developing theatrical skills, the club provides students with a supportive
environment where they can discover their potential, build lasting friendships,
and develop values and life skills that will continue to guide them in
the future.</p>

<p>The Peicai Secondary School Drama Club proudly participated in the International
Science Drama Competition (ISDC), a unique platform that combines the worlds
of Science and Drama. This competition challenged students to creatively
present scientific concepts through theatrical performance, allowing them
to apply both their imagination and critical thinking skills in meaningful
ways. Our Secondary 1 Drama Club members represented the school in this
enriching experience.</p>
<p>The objective of ISDC was not only to deepen students’ understanding of
scientific ideas, but also to develop their confidence, communication skills,
and ability to collaborate effectively as a team. Through the process of
developing the script, rehearsing, and preparing for the performance, students
learnt how to think critically and adapt creatively to challenges. They
also strengthened important E21 competencies such as communication, collaboration,
and adaptive thinking.</p>
<p>Throughout the preparation journey, students demonstrated resilience,
commitment, and teamwork. Drama Club members spent many hours rehearsing
together, supporting one another, and refining their performance as a team.
Although the team did not advance beyond the preliminary rounds, the experience
was still extremely meaningful and rewarding for all involved.</p>
<p>One of the highlights of the competition was seeing our students grow
in confidence as they prepared and recorded their final performance video.
Many students grew in self-confidence and learnt how to express themselves
more effectively through drama. The competition also allowed students to
experience how Drama can be used beyond entertainment, as a powerful tool
for learning, creativity, and communication.</p>
<p>Peicai Drama Club remains committed to providing students with authentic
learning opportunities that nurture confidence, creativity, resilience,
and teamwork. Through platforms such as ISDC, students are able to grow
holistically while discovering their strengths and developing valuable
life skills that extend beyond the classroom.</p>
