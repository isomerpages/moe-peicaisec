<h4><strong>Goals &amp; Objectives</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The motto of Girl Guides is “<strong>Be Prepared</strong>”. We aim to
prepare girls for life through providing opportunities to discover and
develop their strengths and skills to the fullest potential.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We empower our girls to plan CCA activities catered to their needs and
interests and to execute initiatives on societal issues that they care
about. Our girls have reached out to the community around us, making a
positive difference in our society.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We hope that through Girl Guides, we will be able to:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Provide opportunities to help the girls discover and develop their strengths
and skills to the fullest</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop girls to become responsible citizens of the world</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Nurture the girls to be prepared for each opportunity or challenge they
may face</p>
</li>
</ul>
