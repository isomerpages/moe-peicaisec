<h4><strong>Goals &amp; Objectives</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>NCC is guided by 3 main thrusts: Leadership, Fitness and Commitment to
Singapore</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Charting the Course for 21st-Century Leaders we constantly updating our
leadership model to meet evolving needs of the CCA to ensure that everyone
stays engaged, subscribed and committed to the cause of the unit.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fostering Resilient Leaders through Thrilling Adventures such as Kayaking
and Dragon Boating with NCC HQ at Kallang Sea Sports Center</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the end of their journey in NCC, we hope to empower cadets with a "Strong"
mindset. A NCC Cadet is “Cadet Strong”, when he has a <strong>resilient mind, fit body, committed heart</strong> and
is a <strong>self-motivated leader</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>

<p>On 22 May 2026, our NCC (Sea) Unit traded their boots for paddles as they
took to the waters for the highly anticipated Dragon Boat Regatta. Competing
against some of the finest teams from across the nation, our cadets showcased
an incredible display of synchronicity, mental fortitude, and sheer physical
endurance.
<br>
<br>We are immensely proud to announce that our intensive training regimen
bore fruit in a major way: both of our school’s registered boats beat their
previous personal bests, achieving significant improvements in their race
timings (Personal best timing 1:07:71). In a stunning display of power
and teamwork, one of our boats successfully fought its way through the
intense heats to secure a <strong>well-deserved spot in the Semi-Finals</strong>.
Watching our cadets row in perfect harmony, cutting through the water with
determination in every stroke, was an absolute inspiration for everyone
cheering from the riverbanks.
<br>
<br>While medals may be awarded on the podium, character is forged through
perseverance in the water. The resilience demonstrated by our cadets—pressing
on despite physical fatigue and challenging their own limits—reflects the
true spirit and values of the National Cadet Corps. Their determination,
discipline, and unwavering commitment in the face of adversity exemplify
the qualities of exemplary cadets and serve as a testament to their strength
of character.</p>
<p>Congratulations to all our dragon boat racers!</p>

<p>Our National Cadet Corps (NCC) Unit has been awarded the <strong>prestigious Distinction Award</strong> in
the annual National Cadet Corps Unit Recognition. As the highest level
of recognition, this accolade stands as a testament to the unwavering commitment,
discipline, and exemplary teamwork displayed by our cadets and teacher
officers throughout the year. It reflects their collective dedication to
excellence and the steadfast pursuit of the values upheld by the National
Cadet Corps.</p>

<p>The Unit Recognition assessment evaluates NCC units across Singapore on
a stringent set of criteria, including training attendance, leadership
development, participation in national events, and community service initiatives.
From grueling precision drills and adventurous outdoor camps to mastering
essential survival skills, our cadets have consistently pushed their boundaries
and embodied the true spirit of the corps.
<br>
<br>This achievement would not have been possible without the leadership of
our Cadet Leaders, the commitment of our Junior and Senior Cadets, and
the invaluable guidance of our Teacher Officers and Commanding Officer.
Their mentorship has transformed our cadets into resilient, responsible,
and forward-thinking leaders of tomorrow.</p>
<p>To our NCC Unit: Thank you for flying the school flag so high and making
us incredibly proud. Your hard work has truly paid off. We look forward
to watching the unit continue to march from strength to strength in the
coming year.</p>
