<h4><strong>Goals &amp; Objectives</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To enhance the basketball skills of the players which include teaching
fundamental skills like dribbling, shooting, passing and defense, as well
as more advanced techniques as players progress in their journey at Basketball
Boys CCA.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To emphasize the importance of working together as a team, fostering camaraderie
and teaching good sportsmanship; including respect for opponents, officials
and fellow teammates.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop leadership skills in players that will help them take on roles
such as team captain or mentor; helping them develop leadership qualities
that extend beyond the court</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop character of the players by teaching values such as discipline,
perseverance, and responsibility.</p>
</li>
</ol>
