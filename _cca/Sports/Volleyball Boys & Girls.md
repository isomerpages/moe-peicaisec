<h4><strong>Goals &amp; Objectives</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>In Volleyball, we aim to develop (1) Passion for the sport of Volleyball,
(2) Skills &amp; Gameplay competencies and (3) Sportsmanship, teamwork
and values.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Besides learning important volleyball skills, students also learn the
rules, formations and strategies of the game.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Through weekly trainings, students grow into individuals who possess strong
sense of sportsmanship, unwavering resilience and high level of discipline.</p>
</li>
</ol>
