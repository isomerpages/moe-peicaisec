<p>If your child/ward is eligible to take HMTL based on his/her PSLE results,
you would receive an online form on <em>Offer to Take HMTL </em>via Parents
Gateway (PG) or email for those not yet on-board PG <strong>by 22 December 2025 (Mon).</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Parents can accept the offer by giving consent via PG or email by<strong> 12:00pm on 26 December 2025 (Fri).</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>If your child/ward is not eligible to take HMTL based on the PSLE results
but meets the school-based eligibility criteria at the end of Sec 1 Semester
One or the Sec 1 Year End Examinations, an offer would be made by the school
for your child/ward to take HMTL.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Should you have further queries on Higher Mother Tongue Languages, you
could contact Mr Tau Jun Hui (Subject Head, Chinese Language) via email
at <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">tau_jun_hui@moe.edu.sg</a>.</p>
