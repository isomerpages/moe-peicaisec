<p>The MAE only applies to students who wish to request a school transfer
due to&nbsp;<strong>serious medical conditions or severe physical impairments</strong>.</p>
<p>All appeals must be accompanied by relevant supporting documents such
as an official letter from a certified doctor and/or assessment from qualified
medical professionals.</p>
<p>For students who wish to participate in MAE, parents will need to submit
their appeals at Peicai Secondary <strong><u>by 22 December (Mon) at 12:00pm,</u></strong> after
the release of the posting results. Alternatively, you may call the school
at 6202 1260 to request for the Application Form to be emailed to you and
you may return the form with supporting documents via email (<strong>peicai_ss@moe.edu.sg</strong>)
by the same deadline. Verification of parent/guardianâ€™s identity may be
required during the application process.</p>
<p>Appeals will be considered by MOE on a case-by-case basis, and the school
will notify you of the outcome by <strong>end December</strong>. Please
note that school transfers are subject to the availability of vacancies.
Successful applicants will <strong>not</strong> be allowed to reject the
reposted school.</p>
<p>If you need further clarifications on the MAE by MOE, please contact the
school at 6202 1260.</p>
