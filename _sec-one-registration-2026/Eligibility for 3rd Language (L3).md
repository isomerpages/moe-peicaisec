<p>If your child/ward is eligible to take L3 based on his/her PSLE results,
you would receive an online form on <em>Offer to Take L3 </em>via Parents
Gateway (PG) or email for those not yet on-board PG <strong>by 22 December 2025 (Mon).</strong>
<br>The L3s which your child/ward are eligible for will be indicated. If a
particular L3 is not indicated, it means they are not eligible for that
L3.</p>
<p>Students are to indicate the choices of L3 via (i) the S1 Internet System
or (ii) through his/her primary school.</p>
<p>Eligible L3 may include:</p>
<p>● Malay (Special Programme)</p>
<p>● Chinese (Special Programme)</p>
<p>● Arabic</p>
<p>● Bahasa Indonesia</p>
<p>● French</p>
<p>● German</p>
<p>● Japanese</p>
<p>● Spanish</p>
<p>Students who are successfully offered a Third Language are required to
confirm acceptance of the offer and complete registration from Friday,
26 December 2025 to Wednesday, 7 January 2026.</p>
<p>Students assigned to C(SP)/M(SP) zonal centres or MOELC must refer to
the respective zonal centre/MOELC website for registration instructions
to accept the offer. Places will be reallocated to waiting list applicants
if students fail to register by the stipulated date.</p>
<p>Should you have further queries on Third Language programmes, you could
contact Mr Tau Jun Hui (Subject Head, Chinese Language) via email at
<a href="mailto:tau_jun_hui@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">tau_jun_hui@moe.edu.sg</a>.</p>
