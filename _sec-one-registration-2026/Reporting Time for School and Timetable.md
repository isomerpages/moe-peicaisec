<p><strong><em>For Secondary One students, this would be with effect from <u>12 January 2026</u> (Mon) (after the Secondary One Orientation Week Programme).</em></strong>
</p>
<p><strong><u>Monday – Friday</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Students will start assembling in their respective form class at the Parade
square at 7:50 am.</p>
<p>The flag raising ceremony will commence at 7:50 am. Students who are not
present with their form class for the flag raising ceremony at the Parade
square, will be considered as late for school.</p>
<p><strong><u>Drop off / Alighting Points</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The designated drop-off point is the sheltered front porch and students
will proceed to the Parade square on Mondays – Fridays for morning assembly.</p>
<p>In the event of rain, students will proceed to the Multi Purpose Hall
(MPH) for morning assembly.</p>
<p><strong><u>Timetable</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Form Teachers would be issuing students their class timetables by 5 January
2026 (Mon).</p>
<p>This timetable will be used with effect from 12 January 2026 (Mon), after
the Secondary One Orientation Week Programme which will be from 05 January
(Mon) to 9 January 2026 (Fri).</p>
