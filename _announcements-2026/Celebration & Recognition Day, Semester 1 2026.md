<p>In the final week of Term 2 and Semester 1, the school came together to
celebrate our bi-annual Celebration &amp; Recognition Day. This meaningful
occasion recognised students across various levels for their outstanding
academic and non-academic achievements.</p>
<p>Three key academic awards were presented: the All-Round Excellence Awards,
the Co-Curricular Activity (CCA) Awards, and the Principal’s List Award.
The school was also delighted to welcome back our alumni from the Class
of 2025, who were recognised for their achievements in the GCE N-Level
and O-Level examinations, as well as for their holistic contributions during
their time in Peicai.</p>
<p>A highlight of the ceremony was the presentation of the Outstanding Student
of the Year Award 2025. This prestigious award was conferred upon alumnus
Pratheepan Poorni, in recognition of her exemplary character, holistic
achievements, and significant contributions to the school community.</p>

<p>Additionally, the Principal’s List, the most prestigious academic award
in the school, was also awarded to students from all levels. It was introduced
in 2022 to both recognise and commend students for their outstanding academic
performance in the examinations. Award recipients would have had to demonstrate
exemplary conduct to be an awardee of the Principal’s List. In addition
to motivating the students, it is the school’s hope that the students on
the Principal’s List would also be an inspiration to the student body that
nothing is impossible with discipline and hardwork.</p>

<p>Besides students, teaching and non-teaching staff were also recognised
