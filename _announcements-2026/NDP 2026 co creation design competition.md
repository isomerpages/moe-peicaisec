<p>We are proud to share that Misha Marissa Binte Meslan's co-created logo
design has been selected to be featured on the official National Day Parade,
Logo Co-Creation page (<a href="https://www.ndp.gov.sg/going-beyond/ndp-logo-co-creation/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.ndp.gov.sg/going-beyond/ndp-logo-co-creation/</a> )
! This is a wonderful recognition of her creativity and her contribution
to a meaningful national initiative.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The NDP logo co-creation initiative, introduced in 2025, invited individuals
to contribute creatively to segments of the NDP logo. Building on the strong
participation and creative spirit demonstrated last year, this year's edition
extends the invitation to schools to help shape a symbol we collectively
share, which cultivates a deeper sense of pride, ownership, and expression
that reflects who we are and what we stand for.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>In sustaining this initiative, we honour the creativity of our community,
strengthening the connection to the NDP identity and building on the momentum
established, true to the call of <em>"Majulah Singapura, Go Beyond!"</em>
</p>

<p>Selected designs will be progressively unveiled in the lead-up to National
Day, and can be viewed on the official NDP website at <a href="http://www.ndp.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.ndp.gov.sg</a>,
as well as along select LED screens at the National Stadium. We invite
our school community to look out for her work as we celebrate this special
milestone together.</p>
