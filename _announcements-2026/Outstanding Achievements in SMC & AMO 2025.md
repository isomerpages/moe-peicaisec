<p>Peicai Secondary School is proud to celebrate the outstanding achievements
of our students in the <strong>Singapore Math Challenge (SMC) 2025</strong> and
the <strong>American Mathematics Olympiad (AMO) 2025</strong>. Their accomplishments
reflect strong mathematical reasoning, perseverance, and a commitment to
excellence.</p>
<p><strong>Singapore Math Challenge (SMC) 2025</strong>
</p>
<p>The Singapore Math Challenge (SMC) is an international mathematics competition
that develops critical thinking skills and challenges students with rigorous,
problem-solving tasks.</p>
