<p>Their presentation reflected strong scientific curiosity, creativity,
and confidence, as well as the courage to explore new ideas and communicate
them clearly to a wider audience. It was truly heartening to see our students
exemplify the spirit of inquiry, collaboration, and perseverance that lies
at the heart of STEM learning.</p>
<p><strong>Project Title:</strong>
<br><em>Evaluation of Anti-fungal Activity of Dried Flower Extracts Clitoria ternatea, Hibiscus rosa-sinensis and Ixora coccinea on Candida Albicans and Trichophyton Rubrum</em>

<p><strong>Venue:</strong> Singapore Expo</p>
<p>A big congratulations to our young innovators for flying the Peicai flag
high and representing the school with pride.
<br>We warmly invite all students, parents, teachers, and members of the public
to come down to the Expo to show your support and be inspired by their
work!</p>
