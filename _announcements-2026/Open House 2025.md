<h4><strong>Peicai Secondary Open House 2025 – A Celebration of One Peicai Spirit</strong></h4>
<p>Peicai Secondary School’s Open House 2025, held on <strong>Saturday, 22 November 2025</strong>,
was a vibrant and memorable showcase of our school’s spirit, talents, and
community.</p>
<p>The afternoon came alive with <strong>stellar performances</strong> by our <strong>Band, Modern Dance, and Drama</strong> groups,
captivating the audience and reflecting the confidence, creativity, and
passion of our students. Beyond the stage, there was a wonderful buzz of
energy as visitors mingled freely at the <strong>CCA booths</strong> and <strong>Subject Information booths</strong>,
engaging in meaningful conversations with students and teachers while gaining
deeper insights into life at Peicai.</p>
<p>More than just an event, the Open House was a true <strong>One Peicai effort</strong>—made
possible by the dedication, teamwork, and enthusiasm of our students and
staff, who worked tirelessly behind the scenes and on the ground.</p>
<p>A heartfelt <strong>thank you to all students, staff, parents, and visitors</strong> for
making Open House 2025 a resounding success. Your support and presence
truly made the day special, and we look forward to welcoming many new Peicaians
into our family.</p>
