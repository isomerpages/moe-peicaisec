<p>Our National Cadet Corps (NCC) Unit has been awarded the <strong>prestigious Distinction Award</strong> in
the annual National Cadet Corps Unit Recognition. As the highest level
of recognition, this accolade stands as a testament to the unwavering commitment,
discipline, and exemplary teamwork displayed by our cadets and teacher
officers throughout the year. It reflects their collective dedication to
excellence and the steadfast pursuit of the values upheld by the National
Cadet Corps.</p>

<p>The Unit Recognition assessment evaluates NCC units across Singapore on
a stringent set of criteria, including training attendance, leadership
development, participation in national events, and community service initiatives.
From grueling precision drills and adventurous outdoor camps to mastering
essential survival skills, our cadets have consistently pushed their boundaries
and embodied the true spirit of the corps.
<br>
<br>This achievement would not have been possible without the leadership of
our Cadet Leaders, the commitment of our Junior and Senior Cadets, and
the invaluable guidance of our Teacher Officers and Commanding Officer.
Their mentorship has transformed our cadets into resilient, responsible,
and forward-thinking leaders of tomorrow.</p>
<p>To our NCC Unit: Thank you for flying the school flag so high and making
us incredibly proud. Your hard work has truly paid off. We look forward
to watching the unit continue to march from strength to strength in the
coming year.</p>
