<p>During assembly, <strong>Mr Alvin Yeo</strong>, HOD of Physical Education
and CCA, formally announced the start of the <strong>National School Games</strong>,
signalling the beginning of an exciting and challenging sporting season
for our student-athletes. The announcement set an uplifting tone, reminding
students that the National School Games are not just about competition,
but about growth, discipline, and representing the school with honour.</p>

<p>A key highlight of the assembly was the Athletes’ Oath-taking ceremony.
Five student representatives from different sports proudly took the oath
on behalf of the school, affirming their commitment to fair play, integrity,
respect, and perseverance. <strong>Ling Zhi Feng Benson (Volleyball), Loh Jun Yang Anh Tristan (Basketball)</strong>, <strong>Naw Kaira Marcus (Floorball), Lim Ziliang Jasper (Badminton) and Sulaksha Pun (Softball) </strong>stood
confidently before their peers, symbolising unity and leadership across
all sports CCAs.</p>
