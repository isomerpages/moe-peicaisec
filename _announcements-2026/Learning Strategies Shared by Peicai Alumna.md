<p>Peicai Secondary School was delighted to host an alumni sharing session
on campus, featuring our alumna Shankar Kerthana, who graduated in 2023.
She shared practical study tips, insights on maintaining a positive mindset,
and reflections from her transition to Raffles JC.</p>

<p>Kerthana shared how she adapted to the more independent learning environment
at Raffles JC, balancing rigorous academic demands with co-curricular activities.
She spoke about strategies for managing time effectively, staying motivated
under pressure, and seeking support from peers and teachers when needed.</p>

<p>During the talk, Kerthana shared effective study methods tailored to different
subjects. She emphasised the importance of active recall and consistent
practice for content-heavy subjects, while highlighting structured revision,
time management, and reflection for application-based disciplines. Her
tips were grounded in real experiences, offering students clear and actionable
strategies they could adopt immediately.</p>

<p>Beyond academics, Kerthana spoke candidly about cultivating a positive
and resilient mindset. She encouraged students to view challenges as opportunities
for growth, to remain disciplined during demanding periods, and to believe
in steady progress rather than quick results. Her message resonated strongly
with the audience, reminding them that perseverance and self-belief are
just as vital as technique.</p>
