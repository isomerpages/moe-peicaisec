<p>Do you remember an English teacher who deepened your appreciation for
language, helped you think more clearly through reading and writing, or
made lessons engaging and meaningful?</p>
<p>Peicai Secondary School invites students, parents, and alumni to nominate
an English teacher who continually refines their teaching practice, adapts
instructional approaches to meet studentsâ€™ needs, and brings the English
Language curriculum to life through purposeful and engaging classroom experiences.</p>
<p>Share a story. Celebrate an impact. Honour a teacher who inspires a love
for language and learning.</p>
<p>Nominations for the Inspiring Teacher of English Award 2026 are now open.
All in-service English Language teachers, including teachers of Literature
in English, are eligible for nomination.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>How to nominate</strong>
</p>
<p>Please click <a href="https://www.languagecouncils.sg/goodenglish/inspiring-teacher-of-english-award/nomination-information" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to
download the nomination form for the Teaching Award and submit your completed
nomination by <strong>6:00 pm on Monday, 23 March 2026</strong>, via email
to Ms Ng Si Ying, LH Mathematics, at <a href="mailto:ng_si_ying@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">ng_si_ying@moe.edu.sg</a>, or to the
school at <a href="mailto:peicai_ss@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">peicai_ss@moe.edu.sg</a>.</p>
<p>We look forward to celebrating the teachers who have made a lasting difference
in the lives of our students.</p>
