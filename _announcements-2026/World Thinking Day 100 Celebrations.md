<h4><strong>Peicai Girl Guides Commemorate World Thinking Day 100</strong></h4>
<p>The Peicai Girl Guides proudly commemorated the 100th anniversary of World
Thinking Day in February, a significant occasion celebrated by Girl Guides
and Girl Scouts around the world. This special day recognises the values
of friendship, leadership, and global sisterhood that unite Guides across
different countries and cultures.</p>
<p></p>
<p>To mark this milestone, our Guides gathered for a parade and observance
ceremony. The ceremony included the recitation of the Guide Promise and
Law, reminding the Guides of the values and responsibilities that Guides
strive to uphold in their daily lives.</p>

<p>During the observance ceremony, the Guides also reflected on the history
and significance of World Thinking Day and how the movement has empowered
generations of girls to develop confidence, leadership, and a strong sense
of service to the community. It was a meaningful moment for the Guides
to appreciate that they are part of a global network of millions of girls
and women who share the same commitment to making a positive difference
in the world.</p>
