<p><strong>Breakfast with Principal: Welcoming Our Secondary 1 Parents</strong>
</p>
<p>On 28 February 2026, Peicai Secondary School held its annual Breakfast
with Principal event, bringing together Secondary 1 parents for a morning
of meaningful dialogue, connection, and community building. The session
provided a valuable platform for parents to engage directly with Principal
Mr Michael Muhunthan and learn more about the school's Parent Support Group
(PSG), with the aim of strengthening the vital school-parent partnership
that supports every child's journey.</p>
<p>The morning began with an energetic warm-up game facilitated by the PSG
EXCO, where parents responded to lighthearted yet thought-provoking statements
about parenting and raising teenagers. Laughter filled the room as parents
discovered shared experiences and common ground, setting a welcoming tone
for the conversations that followed.</p>

<p>Mr Michael then opened the floor for parents to share their insights,
questions, and observations. The candid exchange allowed school leadership
to hear directly from families, deepening mutual understanding and trust.
Parents raised thoughtful points about their children's transition to secondary
school, and the dialogue underscored the importance of open communication
between home and school in nurturing student growth.</p>

<p>A highlight of the event was the sharing by the PSG EXCO members, who
spoke openly about their personal experiences and the profound impact that
active involvement has had on their appreciation for the school-parent
partnership. Ms Irmah Ayub, Ms Valerie Cheng, Mr Sam Ho, Ms Gina Chow,
and Ms Rubaa Letchumanan shared how their roles in the PSG had not only
strengthened their connection to the school community but also enriched
their relationships with their own children. Their authentic reflections
resonated deeply with attendees, illustrating how parent engagement goes
beyond volunteerism—it becomes a means of walking alongside their children
through the formative secondary school years.</p>

<p>The session concluded with an invitation for parents to join the PSG,
emphasising that every contribution, big or small, makes a difference in
creating a supportive and vibrant school environment. For many Secondary
1 parents, the breakfast served as both a warm welcome into the Peicai
family and an encouragement to take an active role in partnering the school
in ensuring their child's success and enjoyment in Peicai.</p>
