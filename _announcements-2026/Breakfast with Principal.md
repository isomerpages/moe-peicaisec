<p><strong>Breakfast with Principal: Welcoming Our Secondary 1 Parents</strong>
</p>
<p>On 28 February 2026, Peicai Secondary School hosted its annual Breakfast
with Principal, bringing together Secondary 1 parents for a morning of
meaningful dialogue, connection, and community building. The session provided
a valuable opportunity for parents to engage with Principal, Mr Michael
Muhunthan and learn more about the school’s Parent Support Group (PSG),
strengthening the important partnership between home and school.</p>
<p>The morning began with an engaging warm-up activity facilitated by the
PSG EXCO, where parents responded to lighthearted yet thought-provoking
statements about parenting and raising teenagers. The activity sparked
lively conversations and laughter as parents discovered shared experiences
and perspectives.</p>

<p>Mr Michael then opened the floor for parents to share their insights,
questions, and observations. The candid exchange provided an opportunity
for the school leadership to hear directly from families, fostering deeper
mutual understanding and trust. Parents raised thoughtful questions about
their children’s transition to secondary school, and the discussion highlighted
the importance of open communication and strong collaboration between home
and school in supporting students’ growth.</p>

<p>A highlight of the event was the sharing by members of the PSG EXCO, who
spoke openly about their personal experiences and the meaningful impact
of their involvement in the school community. PSG EXCO members, Ms Irmah
Ayub, Ms Valerie Cheng, Mr Sam Ho, Ms Gina Chow, and Ms Rubaa Letchumanan
shared how their participation in the PSG had strengthened their connection
with the school while also enriching their relationships with their children.
Their authentic reflections resonated with many parents, illustrating that
parent engagement goes beyond volunteerism. It is about walking alongside
their children throughout their formative secondary school years.</p>

<p>The session concluded with an invitation for parents to join the Parent
Support Group (PSG), emphasising that every contribution, big or small,
plays a meaningful role in creating a supportive and vibrant school community.
For many Secondary 1 parents, the breakfast served as both a warm welcome
into the Peicai family and an encouragement to take an active role in partnering
the school to support their child’s growth and success.</p>
