<p>One of the most memorable highlights was a heartfelt performance by the
Parent Support Group (PSG). They presented an original song, “You Show
Up,” which they had specially written and composed in honour of our teachers.
The song’s title is a perfect reflection of what teachers do everyday –
showing up with patience, care, and wisdom, rain or shine. Its meaningful
lyrics deeply moved everyone in the hall, leaving our teachers feeling
truly appreciated.</p>
