<p>The objectives of the Secondary One Orientation Programme are as follows:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>to enable Secondary One students to get to know the school environment
and facilities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to know the Form Teachers and classmates better as they make their first
steps to build lasting friendship</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to instill a sense of pride and sense of belonging towards the school</p>
</li>
</ul>
